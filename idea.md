## The Problem

In 2025, the crypto industry suffered catastrophic losses:

- Over **$3.4 billion** stolen in hacks and thefts (Chainalysis)
- North Korean actors alone stole **$2.02 billion**
- Additional **$17 billion** lost to scams, social engineering, and operational failures
- DeFi exploits continue in 2026, with early-year losses already exceeding $137 million across protocols

Even "sophisticated" users fall victim to:
- **Wallet compromises** and private key theft
- **Front-running** and MEV attacks on transparent strategies
- **Human error** — emotional trading, forgotten seed phrases, or missed market crashes
- **Rogue agents** in the emerging agentic economy (AI agents with unchecked privileges)

**Traditional solutions fail the agentic future**:
- Manual DeFi management is exhausting and error-prone
- Transparent on-chain actions expose strategies to front-runners
- Most "security" tools are reactive, not autonomous
- No seamless way to give AI agents safe, revocable power without losing custody

Everyday people and institutions alike live in constant fear: **"What if I get hacked or my assets crash while I sleep?"**

## The Solution

**HALO** is the world's first **Autonomous Smart Firewall** for your digital life — a production-grade guardian that watches, protects, and acts 24/7 while you live normally.

### How HALO Works (User Experience)
1. **Onboard in seconds** — Email / Passkey / Apple/Google login (Flow Account Abstraction — no seed phrases, no gas worries)
2. **One toggle**: "Enable Autonomous Protection"
3. **HALO takes over**:
   - Automatically moves idle funds into safest yield-bearing protocols on Flow
   - Monitors for threats (market crashes, volatility spikes, potential exploits)
   - Confidentially hedges into shielded BTC via Starknet when needed
   - "Ghosts" transactions using privacy layers so no one can track or front-run you
   - Maintains an immutable encrypted audit trail

**For normal users**: It feels like a premium security SaaS (Cloudflare + Apple iCloud+ aesthetics). No crypto jargon. Just peace of mind.

**For power users & agents**: Full transparency via structured execution logs and kill-switch controls.

### Core Architecture — The "Black Hole" Design
Simple, beautiful frontend. Extremely sophisticated backend that seamlessly integrates **every major sponsor technology**:

- **Flow (Consumer DeFi)**: Walletless onboarding + Scheduled Transactions + Cadence for invisible auto-compounding and rebalancing.
- **Zama (Confidential Finance)**: Fully Homomorphic Encryption (fhEVM) for "Confidential Rules" — e.g., "If net worth drops >10%, hedge" — without ever exposing your actual balances or strategy.
- **Protocol Labs Agent Only + ERC-8004**: Swarm of specialized Guardian Agents (monitor, threat detector, executor) with on-chain identities. Agents verify each other to prevent rogue behavior.
- **Lit Protocol (Vincent)**: Programmable signing with strict guardrails — agents can protect funds but can never withdraw to unauthorized addresses.
- **Storacha / Filecoin**: "The Black Box" — permanent, encrypted audit logs and agent memory. Portable across devices and fully immutable.
- **World ID**: Biometric "Dead-Man's Switch" — periodic Orb-verified check-ins. No check-in? Agents autonomously initiate digital inheritance to beneficiaries.
- **Starknet**: Shielded BTC hedging for Bitcoin-grade privacy during high-volatility periods.
